Data Deletion Instructions

If you wish to delete your data associated with the About Jesus Ministry Facebook App, please follow the steps below:

  1. Go to your Facebook account settings.
  2. Click on "Apps and Websites".
  3. Find and click on the app named "About Jesus Ministry".
  4. Click "Remove" to delete the app and all associated data.

Alternatively, you can request data deletion by emailing us at: aboutjesusministry@gmail.com
